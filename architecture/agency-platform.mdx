The Agency Platform provides the multi-tenant layer of Travomatrix, isolating agencies and their users while providing centralized financial control.

## Multi-tenant Design
Every resource in Travomatrix (Bookings, Wallets, Users) is scoped to an `AgentID`. This ensures that one agency cannot view or modify the data of another.

## RBAC System
The Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system is implemented at the API layer. Roles like `SUPER_ADMIN` can perform system-wide reloads and credit adjustments, while `AGENT` roles are restricted to flight operations.

## Wallet Ledger
The ledger is the source of truth for all financial data. The wallet balance is a derived value from the sum of all successful ledger entries, though it is cached in the `wallets` table for performance.

### Schema Overview
- **agents**: Stores company profiles and credit configurations.
- **wallets**: Stores current balances and currency.
- **wallet_transactions**: The immutable ledger of all financial movements.
- **users**: Stores authentication data and role assignments.
