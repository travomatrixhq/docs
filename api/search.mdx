Search for flights with real-time B2B pricing, including automated markup and commission calculations.

### B2B Mode
To enable B2B pricing, ensure the `mode` field is set to `b2b`. This will trigger the `PricingEngine` to calculate:
- **Commission**: Your agency's earnings.
- **Net Fare**: The actual amount that will be deducted from your wallet.
